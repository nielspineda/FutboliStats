FutboliStats

FutboliStats is a website dedicated to advanced statistics for women's soccer players.

We here at FutboliStats believe that statistics are important for the modern-day sports conversation. Unfortunately, there is a significant lack of statistics-more specifically advanced statistics- in women's sports, and especially, women's soccer. Our mission at FutboliStats is to bridge this gap in the sports' world.

Currently, our data is only available due to those hard at work from WoSo Statistics (@WoSoStats). Unfortunately, we understand that there are many matches that still go untracked, and staying up to date in general is difficult with the extensive work that goes into tracking stats. If you would like to help with the data, volunteers are always greatly needed and appreciated!

Data helps tell the story of a player,
a team, an entire career.
Sue Bird
FUTBOLISTATS

All statistics from FutboliStats come from WoSo Statistics (@WoSoStats) and their stat trackers. If you have extra time and would like to contribute, volunteers are always needed for various matches.
